California Dreaming on a Winters Day.

So . . . A little blog article about the EnterMedia trip out west.  I don't think any of us were truly looking forward to the connecting flights from Dayton International to LAX.  I watched the midwest pass plainly below on the first flight from the last row of the plane, which I had entirely to myself.  Something about Frontier airlines:  Our party wasn't seated together on any of the flights.  We had a brief lay-over in Denver, and then we headed over the Rockies, and to what we could only imagine lay beyond.  Only Jason, from JYS Productions knew for sure as this was his fifth LA trip this year.  After we landed we would find out that meant Roscoe's Chicken and Waffles all around, delicious!
