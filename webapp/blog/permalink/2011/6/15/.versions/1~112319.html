EnterMedia FAQ

 

Q. Video Management: - Ability to upload and stream videos?

Yes - Built in video player based on Flowplayer. Built in Amazon and other content distribution for accelerated video playing. Uploading provided by Flash and Java uploaders with progress bars.

 

Q. Ability to convert videos from one format to another on demand?

Yes - Can convert over 200 formats including all major video formats

 

Q. Ability to maintain the video resolution? Videos needs to be of standard format that could be viewed by standard video players.

Yes - Automatic smart letterboxing to provide standard sizing such as HD and iPad formats

 

Q. Ability to index and archive videos Photo Management?

Yes - can associate still any files with videos such as previews, thumbnails, promos or any other media. Thumbnails are auto created from video keyframes. Fully searchable index and archive.

 

Q. Ability to index and archive photos?

Yes - Full support for all photo formats and metadata

 

Q. Ability to edit photos before archiving it (like Photoshop way of editing)?

Yes - Automatic Crop, resize, watermarking built in. Other filter options developed on demand such as gray-scale.


Q. Ability to index and archive PDFs and MS Office documents with textual content based searching Integration with other systems?

Yes - Built in full-text PDF and MS Office text indexing. Built in version control and status based locking. 

 

Q. Please explain the license for your software.

EnterMedia is licensed under a royalty free, perpetual and transferable GNU LGPL license. This allows for the perpetual use, customization, of all EnterMedia libraries, source code, and related technology. EnterMedia does not require client licenses. EnterMedia software is free to download, we charge only for our services and support.
