DPCI Partners with EnterMedia Inc. to Integrate Open Source Digital Asset Management Software

Partnership Enables Companies to Jointly Market and Sell EnterMedia Digital Asset Management Software and Signifies a Commitment from DPCI to Contribute to the EnterMedia Open Source Project

New York, NY - Dec 08, 2010 - DPCI, a provider of integrated technology solutions for organizations that need to publish content to Web, print, and mobile channels, today announced a partnership with EnterMedia Inc., the developer of EnterMedia open source digital asset management software. Together the companies are already collaborating on several enterprise projects that utilize EnterMedia as the central digital asset management platform to build integrated, multi-channel content management and publishing systems.

Recently, DPCI implemented EnterMedia as part of a multi-channel publishing solution at Consumer Search, Inc. – a PRIMEDIA company focused on real estate advertising books. EnterMedia is being used by PRIMEDIA to centrally store all types of digital assets such as ad templates, property images, stock photos, logos, and other PDF documents which are part of a self-service Web portal.

“We are thrilled to establish a relationship with DPCI in a joint effort to market and sell EnterMedia Digital Asset Management,” said Chris Burkey, CEO at EnterMedia Inc. “DPCI’s expertise in integrating digital asset management technologies with content management, workflow management, and multi-channel publishing systems will help expand the capabilities of the EnterMedia platform into organizations within a number of vertical markets.”

With this partnership, DPCI also signifies its intention to contribute enhancements and modules to the EnterMedia digital asset management project. The company has already integrated EnterMedia digital asset management software with various products including Adobe InDesign Server, Adobe LiveCycle, DataPlan Journal Designer, and a variety of Microsoft application platform technologies.

“At DPCI we have been monitoring the options in open source digital asset management for a number of years,” explained Joe Bachana, CEO of DPCI. “EnterMedia represents the first solution that we feel has matured to a point where it can be implemented for our enterprise customers. Furthermore, we find the team at EnterMedia to be immensely professional, responsive, and helpful during every implementation. These things, along with the ever-growing functionality and development integration points that EnterMedia offers, makes this a perfect fit for our customers that are committed to implementing open source software solutions.”

About EnterMedia

EnterMedia is an open source software company and creator of EnterMedia Digital Asset Management. A full-featured, enterprise-quality software platform, EnterMedia is designed to create and publish large, centralized rich media libraries to increase maximum efficiency and monetary investment. EnterMedia's clients include those needing to manage and distribute online video libraries, implement streamlined workflows in production environments and centralize rich media content locally as well as globally.
