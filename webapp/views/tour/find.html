  • FindEnterMedia is designed around a simple, yet powerful search interface.

    View search results on your terms. Use EnterMedia the way you want to. The search results come back in either list or gallery view, or you can dive into a single asset's details. Controlling your view helps make sorting through results quick.

    Automatically keep track of your previous searches. Every time you search, EnterMedia automatically updates your query to the sidebar for later access. You then have options to save and refine your searches, so you can find the same files today, as you found yesterday, without starting over.

    Refine saved searches to pinpoint the files you want. By refining your search to a specific category or metadata field, you can return the specific files your are looking for. We built the search function to give you extra power, without sacrificing usability.

    Organize with catalogs, categories, tags, and custom fields. Find the media you are searching for quickly with EnterMedia's robust metadata capabilities. We realize that each organization is unique so we buld EnterMedia to be flexible and powerful. The power of EnterMedia lies in the ability to fully customize metadata fields no matter what your workflow entails.

  • ShareDistribute your content, with your branding, without sacrificing file security.

    Automatic thumbnails and conversions. When someone uploads a video asset, EnterMedia will automatically generate thumbnails and conversions behind the scenes. Of course, none of this affects your original files should you need them, and using previews can dramatically reduce bandwidth consumption, so your viewers will spend less time waiting.

    Bundle attachments to the original asset. You can easily bundle additional files to an asset allowing other users to find source files or extras that go with the original asset. This combined with EnterMedia's already powerful search interface means you will spend less time hunting.

    Share files securely. With EnterMedia you can control every aspect of permissions with groups and users. You can totally customize who has the power to view, edit, upload and download your files. There are plenty of ways to quickly manage users across all departments.

  • CommunicateFrom single files to entire collections, EnterMedia makes for easy collaboration.

    Powerful commenting made easy. Built-in commenting in EnterMedia makes taking notes a cinch without obstructing your natural work-flow. This makes it easy for you to clear up any questions your client or team might have, AND since comments are stored with the original asset, everyone knows where to go for the latest information.

    Geo-tag your assets. EnterMedia is a powerful metadata editor. With the geo-tagging feature, you can search by zip code, and quickly give your asset latitude and longitude. That information is bundled with the asset when people view it later.

    Easily attach and send files by email. EnterMedia makes it easy to share files with colleagues across departments without losing track of data. It automatically writes a generic email for you and includes the appropriate links for the files you have selected. Easily customize what size or type of file to send.

    Track file usage and history. Every file action that takes place on your server is recorded and attached to your asset through the system. You can get a read out of users and see the file actions they performed with a time record.
