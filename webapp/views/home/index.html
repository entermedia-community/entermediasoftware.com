Welcome to EnterMedia 8

Don't be shackled to another company to promote your own media. With EnterMedia 8 you can create your own branded media sharing website similar to YouTube or Flickr using Open Source technologies.
Private

If you dont want to share your media with others, you don't have to. EnterMedia runs on your private and secure server allowing you to fully customize the permissions of any visitor to your EnterMedia site.

Integrated

Sometimes, it's nice to be in control. EnterMedia can be customized to blend seamlessly with you existing site, giving you the power to host your own media in a controlled and branded environment with no advertising. Unless, of course, you want advertising.

Scalable

We've created a monster. EnterMedia has the flexibility neccessary to provide incredible digital asset management for small teams that work remotely and need a place to collaborate online, while at the same time being powerful enough to allow that small team to grow into a megacorporation that has a global reach and global impact without ever worrying about outpacing their digital asset management toolset.

EnterMedia has the capability of clustering meaning that it can be installed on an infinite number of servers that can combine processing power and memory and safe guards against one node crashing.
