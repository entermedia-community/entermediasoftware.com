Welcome to EnterMedia 8

With EnterMedia 8 you can create your own branded media sharing website similar to YouTube or Flickr using Open Source technologies.
Secure Permissions

With EnterMedia you can fully customize permissions to grant or restrict user access to files and features. Additionally, EnterMedia tracks and stores history for each asset so that you stay informed about who does what with your media. The management of content as well as groups and roles has been built into the front end for quick and easy administration.

Integrated Environment

Switching over to EnterMedia doesn't mean losing the look and feel of your current site. Our interface can be customized to blend seamlessly with you existing layout. We give you the power to host your own media in a controlled and branded environment that will already feel like home to your users, or like 127.0.0.1 to your technical team.

Expandable Codebase

EnterMedia easily keeps pace with your company's growth. One EnterMedia installation can host as many catalogs or instances as you need, so you never need to worry about outgrowing your server platform. Each department can expand their collection of assets without cluttering or compromising the sovereignty of another division.
