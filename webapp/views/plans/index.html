The Open Source Philosophy

There are many popular open source software programs on the market today. From OpenOffice, which is handy replacement for Microsoft's Office suite, to Linux, a full blown operating system, open source is a fantastic alternative to propriety software. Having the source code freely available encourages collaboration and development among a greater population of software engineers. Ultimately this leads to a community of developers who can create a variety of applications based off the original code and extends the features of the original platform.

The implications of a community of developers working to enhance EnterMedia range from third party extensions to custom connectors to other systems. Propriety software, on the other hand, seems too restrictive with the end-users left waiting and hoping that their feature request will be addressed in upcoming releases. Having the source code openly available gives our clients more flexibility to design and implement a system that addresses their particular requirements.

With over ten years developing digital asset management technologies, we know that some clients appreciate being able to take a more active role in development which is exactly what "open-source," entails. At the same time, other clients are looking for an "out of the box solution, which is why we have included all of the most frequently requested features in our latest release of EnterMedia - right out of the box.

Standard Installation

The standard installation of EnterMedia is suited for companies who require a single server. For departments that require multiple nodes, check out our enterprise installation. The standard installation includes setting up the new system and importing your existing content, both delivered with hands on training during the process. Additional support can be scheduled should it be required in addition to the inclusive training.

EnterMedia specialist will install and setup EnterMedia 8 along with Tomcat, OpenOffice extension for Microsoft formats, and FFmpeg, all on a single server. EnterMedia 8 includes three core applications that will be enabled on your server: Enterprise Media Library, EnterMedia Share, and EnterMedia Manager. Once your system is up and running technicians will link to your digital file hierarchy and import your existing metadata, groups, and users. Dynamic permissions will be enabled, and EnterMedia will be branded with your companies logo and colors.

Finally, our trained specialists will make sure you have the knowledge to implement your new system with confidence. A final status and sign-off report at the end of installation will be provided for your technical records.

Enterprise Installation

The enterprise installation of EnterMedia is design for departments that require multiple nodes with fail-over and scalable search capabilities. Installation includes setting up the new system and importing your existing content. More importantly our enterprise clients are carefully guided through the process by our technicians. If you require additional help in the future EnterMedia offers discounted packages for extended support.

Our specialist will install and setup EnterMedia 8 with Tomcat, OpenOffice extension for Microsoft format, FFmpeg, and ElasticSearch on multiple (up to 3) nodes to form your computing cluster. EnterMedia 8 includes three core applications that will be enabled: Enterprise Media Library, EnterMedia Share, and EnterMedia Manager. Once your system is up and running technicians will link to your digital file hierarchy and import your existing metadata, groups, and users. Dynamic permissions will be enabled, and EnterMedia will be branded with your companies logo and colors.

Before we make the final hand-off, our trained specialists will make sure you have the knowledge to implement your new system with confidence. A final status and sign-off report at the end of installation will be provided for your technical records.
