The Open Source Hourly Assistance

There are many popular open source software programs on the market today. From OpenOffice, which is a strong replacement for Microsoft's Office suite, to Linux, a full blown operating system; open source is a fantastic alternative to propriety software. Having the source code freely available encourages collaboration and development among a greater population of software engineers. Ultimately this leads to a community of developers who can create a variety of applications based off the original code and extends the features of the original platform.

The implications of a community of developers working to enhance EnterMedia range from third party extensions to custom connectors from other systems. Propriety software, on the other hand, seems too restrictive with the end-users left waiting and hoping that their feature request will be addressed in upcoming releases. Having the source code openly available gives our clients more flexibility to design and implement a system that addresses their particular requirements.

Entermedia's ten years experience developing digital asset management technologies, includes client feedback and the ablilty to take a more active role in development.

 

Mash Up Installation

Our Mash Up installation is the perfect way for developers to access our source code, so they can see first hand the potential power of the EnterMedia digital asset management system. EnterMedia is open source so the code is freely available and we want to do our best to encourage development by interested parties.

Standard Installation

The standard installation of EnterMedia is suited for companies who require a single server. For departments that require multiple nodes, check out our enterprise installation. The standard installation includes setting up the new system and importing your existing content, both delivered with hands on training during the process. Additional support can be scheduled should it be required in addition to the inclusive training.

EnterMedia specialist will install and setup EnterMedia 8 along with Tomcat, OpenOffice extension for Microsoft formats, and FFmpeg, all on a single server. EnterMedia 8 includes three core applications that will be enabled on your server: Enterprise Media Library, EnterMedia Share, and EnterMedia Manager. Once your system is up and running technicians will link to your digital file hierarchy and import your existing metadata, groups, and users. Dynamic permissions will be enabled, and EnterMedia will be branded with your companies logo and colors.

Finally, our trained specialists will make sure you have the knowledge to implement your new system with confidence. A final status and sign-off report at the end of installation will be provided for your technical records.

A professional EnterMedia consultant will work with you remotely to:
  • Setup EnterMedia webapp on a single server with Entermedia Manager, Library and Share applications enabled
  • Setup Tomcat webapp on a single server.
  • Setup OpenOffice extension for Microsoft formats.
  • Setup FFmpeg on a single server.
  • Setup and configure EnterMedia 8.0 features.
  • Link to digital file hierarchy.
  • Import Metadata.
  • Import Groups and Users.
  • Enable dynamic permissions.
  • Brand your EnterMedia installation with your organizations logo and colors.
  • Provide Hand's on training and best practices advice throughout installation.
  • Provide EnterMedia status and sign-off report at end of installation for your technical records.
  • Be There Should you need continued support or training after installation, Entermedia provides additional training and server support on an hourly basis or discounted prepaid packages.
